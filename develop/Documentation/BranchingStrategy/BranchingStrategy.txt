ReleaseTypes:

	Release types are seen as a variables in apy's.  They describe how an installer should show up in the Hydra Download Center
	Release types are read via Regex.  General pattern [Actor]-[SourceBranch]-[Environment]

		Dev:				[Developer]-[Feature]-dev
		Int:				Developer-[develop]-dev
		QA:					Tester-[develop]-qa
